01 About

We build the quiet infrastructure behind homes, farms & businesses — reliably, and in full.

At WATPAC Investments, we focus on delivering custom solar solutions tailored to each client's unique energy demands. Our team designs efficient systems that maximize output and reduce costs — from large commercial installations to schools, clinics, and private homes.

Beyond solar, we specialize in borehole drilling and installation — providing sustainable water sources for irrigation, industry, and daily life. And for residential or commercial electrical work, our certified electricians deliver precision installations that meet every safety standard.
