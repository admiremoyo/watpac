About

WATPAC SOLAR AND BOREHOLES, we focus on delivering custom solar solutions tailored to meet the unique energy requirements of each client. Our expert team works closely with you to design an efficient solar system that maximizes energy output and minimizes costs. Whether for a large commercial project or a smaller installation for educational institutions or hospitals, our bespoke approach ensures reliability and sustainability in your energy production.

Our services extend beyond just solar systems as we also specialize in borehole drilling and installation. Boreholes provide a sustainable water source essential for various applications such as irrigation, industrial processes, or drinking water supply. We utilize advanced equipment and techniques to ensure successful borehole installations that contribute positively to your resource management efforts while ensuring compliance with local regulations

At WATPAC INVESTMENT we pride ourselves on offering expert electrical installation services for residential and commercial properties. Our team of certified electricians has extensive experience handling various installations from lighting fixtures to complete wiring systems. We take the time to understand your specific needs ensuring that every installation meets safety standards and is completed efficiently. With our skilled professionals, you can trust that your electrical projects will be handled with precision and care.
